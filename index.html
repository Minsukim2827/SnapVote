About

Welcome to SnapVote! a simple and intuitive website where you can easily design and share your own polls. Choose a duration of 2, 5, or 10 minutes for your poll to be active. Once you create a poll, a unique link will be generated for others to vote and see the real-time results. Let's engage in lively discussions and gather valuable insights through interactive polls.
